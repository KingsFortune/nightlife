# Sound Files

## Double Jump Sound

Place your double jump sound effect here as `doublejump.ogg`

**Requirements:**
- Format: `.ogg` (Ogg Vorbis audio)
- Recommended: Short sound effect (0.2-0.5 seconds)
- Volume: Pre-normalized to avoid being too loud

**Example sound types:**
- Sci-fi "whoosh" effect
- Cybernetic activation sound
- Energy boost sound
- Jet propulsion sound

The sound will play automatically when you double jump in mid-air.
